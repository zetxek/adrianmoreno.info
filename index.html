Who am I?

First - online you will find me as zetxek, as that is my online nickname. Professionally, I am an "all-round" coder that feels comfortable both in technical and management positions, working side by side with his team. I like being close to the technical details of the solutions while keeping an overview of the business cases that we try to solve with technology.

I need to understand the frictions we try to mitigate and why it is important before we jump directly to add technology to the equation.

My main area of focus lies in web and mobile projects - anything connected can start to spark my interest. I like to have the time to care about the details of the implementation, user journey and UX, while having the chance to get a higher level view and observe how the different parts involved connect to each other (via APIs, databases or any other interface), to solve whatever problem we try for our users.
